About the Artist

Since 2011, I have consistently focused on creating figurative sculptures. Based in my hometown of Shiogama, I explore new forms of human expression through sculpture. Recently, I have moved away from idealized forms and have begun to focus on capturing the individuality and inner presence of each model through portraiture.
